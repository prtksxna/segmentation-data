k-means

Calculating kmeans. We aren't including English Wikipedia, Commons and Wikidata in this set because they always skew the data in weird ways.

We are calculating kmeans by taking all numeric data points as a dimension. There are about 30 of those, so that makes it hard to visualize the clustering. So, we are look at the variables analysis and plot on the variables that have the highest coefficient of variation. These are monthly_unique_devices, content_pages and monthly_editors. Plotting these against each other we get 9 scatter plots, moving and zooming in each will move and zoom the others on the same axis as well.

Finding the appropriate number of clusters was a bit tricky, and so for the time being I have generated the data for 3-7 clusters. You can see the visualizations and lists of wikis for each below.
